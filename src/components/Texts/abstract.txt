Transparency is a tool that allows humans to assess the environmental, social and economic credentials of a hyper-human. 
With the help of machine learning the consumer will be able to check, not only what company the hyper-human owns, but also support humans to make a decision about purchasing from him based on how the companys responds to environmental, social and economic issues. The algorithm aims to trawl the internet, other databases and indexes to rate the hyper-human performance on the three issues. This dissertation is proposing to define what is a hyper-human and explore a possible visual that can track and encompass all the corporation and Hyper-human is invested in using open-source-investigation.
