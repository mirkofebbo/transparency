Transparency is a tool that allows humans to assess the environmental, social and economic credentials of a hyper-human by placing the company's logo over your phone or augmented reality glasses. 
With the help of machine learning the consumer will be able to check, not only what company the hyper-human owns, but also support them to make a decision about purchasing from him based on how his company responds to environmental, social and economic issues. The algorithm aims to trawl the internet, other databases and indexes to rate the hyper-human performance on the three issues
