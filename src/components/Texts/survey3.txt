When adding all the sectors together it reveals that people feel mostly “somewhat aware” between the different industries, which would indicate a general knowledge on the issues. Another interesting phenomenon to observe is that the awareness of the transport industry is the highest, as at first glance it could be due to the environmental impact, but the previous graph shows that people are quite aware of this industry's economic impact. 

Regarding the people's willingness to access this kind of information it is easy to see that people perceive that this kind of service should be free, or that they do not care about the product they are buying as the graph is exponentially decreasing, with less than 50% of the people interested to pay for it.
