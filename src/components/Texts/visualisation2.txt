Besides humans ' minimal environmental awareness there is to account for the shorter attention span, from a 2015 study it was 8 seconds one second lower than the goldfish (Kevin McSpadden). Thus how does one encompass a hyper-object as big as the Bill Gates empire within a visual interpretation that can take less than 8 seconds to understand.

The solution we are proposing shows similarity with the Coxcomb diagram made in 1858 by Florence Nightingale to highlight unnecessary deaths during the Crimean War as a result of preventable infections due to poor hygiene habits. In a Coxcomb chart, each category is represented by a section of the disc, and each section has the same angle. The area of a section represents the value of the corresponding category. The problem with it is that some information can be hard to see. They shine when used to show patterns instead of precision.
